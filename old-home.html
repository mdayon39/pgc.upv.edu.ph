Philippine Genome Center Visayas is a research institution that serves as a hub for collaborative genomic research. Leveraging our strengths in genome sequencing, our mission is to advance genomic science, its application to agricultural and medical research, and spearhead the utilization of omics in fisheries and aquatic research in the Philippines.
